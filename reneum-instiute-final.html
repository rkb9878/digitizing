HOW RENEUM WORKS

Reneum finances renewable energy projects in emerging markets

Reneum takes electricity generation data directly from renewable energy projects registered with us, such as a solar PV or a wind plant, in real time, using the generators’ independently-managed monitoring systems. The registration of renewable energy projects is a simple onboarding process, after which projects are entitled to RENW tokens as long as they generate clean energy.

RENWs are certified via a robust assessment process including compliance with global REC standard criteria. The Reneum tokens will be issued free-of-charge to generators of renewable energy worldwide, helping them capitalize their renewable energy projects.

We are buying lagging markets by showing the true demand for renewables globally.

THE PLATFORM

1. Power plant data will be gathered in real-time by a local communication protocol converter or monitoring system.

Independently-owned monitoring systems ensure that no data tampering or fraudulent reporting of generation can occur.

2. 2. Real-time renewable generation is transmitted directly to Reneum’s blockchain database, is automatically verified for accuracy, and then issues a smart contract that cannot be altered or deleted.

3. Tokens called GREN are then issued to renewable energy owners on the Ethereum blockchain on a monthly basis.

4. GREN tokens are available for purchase on decentralized exchanges by any buyer desiring to invest in the renewable energy transition!
